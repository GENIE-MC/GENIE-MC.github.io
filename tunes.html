GENIE comprehensive model configurations and tunes

This section contains a summary description of GENIE tunes and comprehensive model configurations and provides links to tuning outputs (best-fit parameters, covariances) as well as detailed quantitative characterizations of tunes against an extensive collection of data.

Details for the physics configurations and tunes, as well as selected data/MC comparisons may be found in the Physics & User manual. The complete list of data/MC comparisons characterizing each tune will be linked in the table below in due time.
