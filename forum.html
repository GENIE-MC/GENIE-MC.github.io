GENIE User Forum
The GENIE User Forum is intended as a regular meeting where users and core developers can exchange feedback on GENIE. It is typically held monthly, on the third Wednesday of each month at 15:00 GMT. The schedule may occasionally be adjusted to accommodate participants from different time zones.

Agendas for all meetings are publicly accessible at the GENIE document database.

The structure of the forum may vary depending on attendance and requested contributions. However, the typical format is as follows:
  • GENIE status report
  • Progress reports from selected GENIE incubator projects
  • GENIE liason / experiment reports
  • Special invited talks
  • Discussion / AOB

Experiment updates are shared in a roundtable format, with each participating experiment represented by a dedicated liaison. We currently have liaisons from all major running experiments, as well as those about to begin data taking.

To ask for a talk or other questions, please write to the forum moderator.
